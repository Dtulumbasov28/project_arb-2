Protection of Children's Privacy

Our Service does not cater to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you learn that your child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us. If we find out that we have collected Personal Data from children without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.

Should we need to collect information from children under the age of 13, we will do so only with parental consent or as permitted by law.

We do not control, and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.
